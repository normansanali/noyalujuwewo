Career

Acting

Alizeh Shah made her first television appearance at the age of 6. She was featured in a television commercial along with Wasim Akram. In 2017, Alizeh Shah made her acting debut with the Pakistani television drama ‘Tere Naal Love Ho Gaya.’ Later, she was seen playing a supporting role in the drama series Choti Si Zindagi.

Alizeh Shah played her first lead role in “Hoor Pari,” a drama series that aired on A-Plus Entertainment in 2018. Her performance as Gul Naz was very much appreciated by the audience. Later, she became part of many blockbuster television series like Daldal, Tere Naal Luv Hogaya, Dil Mom Ka Diya, Bisaat e Dil, Jo Tu Chahey, and many more.

In 2019, Alizeh Shah made her film debut with the movie Superstar in which she appeared as Chutki. Her performance in the movie grabbed a lot of attention towards her work for which she was nominated at the Pakistan International Screen Awards for Best Supporting Actress.

Singing

Alizeh Shah began her singing career with a Punjabi song “Badnamiyan.” She sang this song along with the popular Punjabi singer of the region Sahir Ali Bagga. The song was Premiered on 15 May 2021 on YouTube.
