Jimmie Herrod Career

The upcoming star was a born singer. When he was in his teenage years, he decided to opt for his career in professional singing. For this, he had to split his time between Portland, Oregon, and Paris, France.

Initially, he started working at theatres, as to start from somewhere to reach the top. He worked in Oregon Symphony’s showcase in the 2018 TEDx series, being featured as a soloist, and worked with international acts such as ODESZA.

Jimmie is currently on the tour and is a permanent member of ‘Pink Martini’. He toured with the club throughout the US, Canada, and Europe.

AGT

Recently, he participated in a popular reality tv show ‘America’s Got Talent, where he conceived standing ovation from judges for his extremely melodious performance, where he sang ‘Tomorrow’ by singer Annie.

The golden buzzer sent Jimmie directly to the live performances. It was quite challenging for him as Simon told him that the song which he was going to perform is the worst song in the world for him.

In addition to this, Simon also advised him to perform any other song but he said that’s the only song which he prepared for.

Despite the negativity, he literally impressed Simon and the rest judges along with the present audiences with his vocal capabilities.

As a reward for his mesmerizing performance, Simon gave him Golden Buzzer which advances him into the quarterfinals for live performance. During the quarterfinals, he performed “Pure Imagination” and as expected, he just killed it and received a standing ovation from not only the audience but from all four judges as well.
