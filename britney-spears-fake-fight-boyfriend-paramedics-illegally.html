Britney Spears Speaks Out About Reports Of A Fight With Her New Boyfriend

After reports came out that paramedics had shown up to rescue Britney Spears from a violent fight with her new boyfriend, fans were beyond worried. The pop star allegedly had gotten into a physical fight with Paul Soliz at her hotel, and when paramedics appeared, rumors started going around about a mental breakdown.

Seemingly back home, Britney has taken the time to ease fans' minds and clarify what actually happened, and she was very upset at the rumors.

"Just to let people know... the news is fake!!! I would like respect at this time for people to understand I am getting stronger every day!!! Truth sucks so can someone teach me how to lie?" She wrote on her Instagram.

Despite the reports apparently being fake, she still said she needed fans' support. "Goddesses out there, I'm reaching my higher power and furthermore, I hope you guys are too!!!"

Britney Spears finished her rant by explaining the real reason she needed medical attention and bashing the paramedics for their actions.

"I also twisted my ankle last night and paramedics showed up at my door illegally. They never came in my room but I felt completely harassed." She concluded by announcing "I'm moving to Boston!!! Peace."

It seems that, while Britney is still going through a lot, she is safe and fans don't need to worry so much about her.
