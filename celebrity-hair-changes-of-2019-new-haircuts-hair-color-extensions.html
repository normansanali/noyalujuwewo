The Best Celebrity Hair Transformations of 2019 — Including Lady Gaga, Hilary Duff, Charlize Theron and More!

If there’s one thing worth taking away from 2019, it’s that we had an amazing year full of celebrity hair changes. From Lady Gaga’s transformation from blonde, to blue and then pink to that time when Kendall Jenner hit the Burberry runway with blonde locks, there was no shortage of buzzworthy looks to go around.

Surely, nothing has the power to transform a look quite like a new hairstyle, which might explain why celebrities are constantly switching things up with fresh cuts, colors and lengths. To ring in the new year, celebrities like Hilary Duff, Khloé Kardashian and Katherine Langford debuted strikingly new hair colors. Duff and Kardashian went icy blonde and Langford went from chestnut brown to a red-orange shade.

One of the biggest hair trends of the year was the bob. A-listers like Lucy Hale, Selena Gomez and Kristen Bell opted for blunt shoulder-length styles, whereas Kylie Jenner, Leighton Meester and Sarah Hyland went with longer and less-drastic versions of the cut.

And the most unexpected trendy hairstyle of 2019 was hands-down the bowl cut. Celebrities somehow made the cut look cool and dare we say, sexy. Two iconic chops worth noting are Charlize Theron’s 'do, inspired by supermodel Linda Evangelista's look from the ‘90s. Kaia Gerber followed in the star’s footsteps, revealing the new look on Instagram with the caption, “Can't seem to keep my hair on my head.”
