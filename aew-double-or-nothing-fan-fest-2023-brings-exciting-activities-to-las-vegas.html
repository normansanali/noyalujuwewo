AEW Double or Nothing Fan Fest 2023 Brings Exciting Activities to Las Vegas

August 2024 · 2 minute read

AEW has announced the highly anticipated AEW Double or Nothing Fan Fest 2023, taking place in Las Vegas. The event will be held at the MGM Grand Conference Center in the Grand Ballroom on Saturday, May 27th, starting at 10:00 AM PDT and running until 6:00 PM PDT. The stage shows will begin at 10:30 AM, offering fans a full day of thrilling entertainment.

The AEW Double or Nothing Fan Fest will feature a range of exciting activities for wrestling fans. Attendees can enjoy various stage shows, including Jazwares, Hey!(EW), All Elite Arcade, AEW Unrestricted Podcast, and more. These shows promise to provide unique insights, engaging discussions, and exclusive behind-the-scenes content for fans to enjoy.

In addition to the stage shows, fans will have the opportunity to meet and greet their favorite AEW talent. A selection of AEW talent Meet & Greets will be available for purchase as an add-on to the general admission ticket. This presents a fantastic chance for fans to interact with their favorite wrestlers, capture memorable moments, and obtain autographs or photos.

It is important for attendees to familiarize themselves with the event’s rules and restrictions. Personal item limits will be strictly enforced, and any violation of the stated restrictions may result in removal from the event without a refund. Ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience for all participants is a priority for AEW.

The AEW Double or Nothing Fan Fest 2023 will be held at the Grand Ballroom of the MGM Grand, located at 3799 South Las Vegas Boulevard, Las Vegas, NV 89109. Tickets for the event can be purchased here.

Don’t miss out on this incredible opportunity to immerse yourself in the world of AEW and join fellow fans in celebrating the passion and excitement of professional wrestling
