Salman Rushdie Biography

Salman Rushdie was born on Thursday, 19 June 1947, in Mumbai, India. He happens to be the only son of Anis Ahmed Rushdie (father) and Negin Butt (mother). He has three sisters and a brother whose names are yet to be made public.

Salman Rushdie attended Cathedral and John Connon School, Bombay Rugby School, Warwickshire. After completing, he went to King’s College, the University of Cambridge to further his education.

Why Is Salman Rushdie So Famous?

Salman Rushdie became famous after the publication of his fifth book, The Satanic Verses, which prompted a fatwa against him in 1989.

What Were Salman Rushdie’s Injuries?

Salman Rushdie was the victim of a ‘brutal attack’ while preparing to give a lecture in western New York state in August 2022. He was stabbed during the incident. He suffered damage to his liver and to nerves in one arm leading to the loss of the use of a hand and sight in one eye.
