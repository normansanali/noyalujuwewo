Anok Yai is a popular Egyptian-American model and social media personality. Anok shot into fame when her picture on Instagram went viral.

Early Life

Anok Yai was born on December 20, 1997, and she is currently 26 years old. She was born in Cairo, Egypt. Her family moved to Machester, New Hampshire during her childhood. Anok is American by nationality and South Sudanese by ethnicity. Similarly, her birth sign is Sagittarius and she follows Christianity.

Talking about her family details, Anok hasn’t shared the names of her parents. However, her father is an employee at Easterseals and her mother is a nurse. Similarly, she also has a sister named Alim Yai, who is a financial consultant.

Education

Moving towards her educational background, Anok completed her schooling at Manchester High School West. Later, she joined Plymouth State University intending to be a doctor and graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in biochemistry. Anok has been talented in studying since her childhood.

Anok Yai – Professional Life and Career

In October 2017, Anok was discovered by a professional British photographer named “Steven Hall” during Howard University’s homecoming week. The photographer clicked a beautiful picture of her which she posted on her Instagram handle. Her photo went viral very soon which created a big sensation on social media. The picture received over 20k likes on the first day.

This popularity helped her to begin her career in the field of modeling. Soon after Anoko started getting calls and offers from several big modeling agencies like Next Model Management and IMG Models. However, Anok started working with Next Model Management.

Within 4 weeks, Anok became the first South Sudanese model to open a Prada fashion show. Similarly, she is also the second black model after the famous model Naomi Campbell. Prada show has launched so many careers that it is considered the highest feat in fashion modeling. In addition to this, Anok has also modeled in the Prada SS 2018 campaign. She has also appeared in the Nike campaign which is designed by Givenchy’s Riccardo Tisci.
