Together, Albert and Deidre have had four children. Deidre actually had another child named Isabella before she met Albert, but Albert took her in as his own.

All seemed to be going well for the couple until opening Day for the 2022 Cardinals. With the return of Albert and a destined playoff spot, many fans were hyped for the upcoming season. However, he shocked fans by announcing his divorce from Deidre around the same time. But that wasn't the only thing shocking about the timing of Albert's decision to call their marriage off. It was also just days after Deidre had surgery for brain tumor removal.

Following Albert's filing, Deidre didn't say too much publicly regarding the divorce. What she did say was mostly positive, like when she announced her unconditional support for her husband on the baseball field. On the flip side, she has also requested Albert pay up for child support.

2 Why Did Albert And Deidre Pujols Divorce After 20 Years Together?

According to the divorce documents, Albert listed their separation date as February 16th, 2022. Like a lot of information involving Deidre, she hasn't publicly revealed much. However, Albert claimed in the initial divorce papers that they were splitting for "irreconcilable differences". In a public statement, Deidre asked for privacy for both her and her family. Otherwise, neither Albert nor Deidre have released ay further details about what led to the dissolution of their marriage.

The exes went on to quickly (and quietly) settle their divorce in August 2023. They agreed that Deidre will keep their kids 70% of the time, and she'll receive nearly $20,000 in monthly child support. Albert also agreed to give their adult daughter Isabella $5,000 a month until he can earn a living on her own. As per the divorce agreement, Albert will retain ownership of several of their properties as well as possession of his car collection.

So much for heartbreak, as Albert's already moved on from his marriage. Since late 2020, he's been in a relationship with a woman named Nicole Fernandez. She's not that famous in the sense that she doesn't end up on magazine covers, but she is a highly famous and influential person in other parts of the world. This is because she is actually the daughter of Leonel Fernandez, the former president of the Dominican Republic. He was seen as highly influential, and had praises from President Obama.

It appears that Albert and Nicole remain happily together, while his ex-wife is living her newfound single life out of the spotlight.
