Introduction :

  • Elie Mystal is an attorney who is now the managing editor of Above the Law, and the Editor-at-Large of Breaking Media.
  • A published author, Elie is also a contributing writer for The Nation, and has written for several publications and appeared on several networks.

Personal Life, Parents and Family Details :

  • Elie Mystal was born in 1978.
  • Elie’s dad, Elie Mystal Senior, who is now deceased, was the first elected African American legislator in the Suffolk County district. His mother’s name is Elizabeth Ying. Elizabeth was born in 1950 in Mississippi.
  • In August 2004, Mystal got married to his wife, Christine Nyereyegonaand. The couple has two children.

Education : Qualifications, High School & College Info

  • Elie moved to Indiana during his senior year of high school.
  • In 2000, Mystal graduated from Harvard University with a Bachelor Degree in Government.
  • He went on to earn his Juris Doctor (JD) from Harvard Law School in 2003.

Career, Income, Salary and Net Worth :

  • After graduating from law school, Mystal began working at the law firm of Debevoise & Plimpton in New York as a litigator.
  • He soon decided to leave the prestigious law firm. The months following his resignation were not easy. Mystal admitted that he spent many days just being on the couch.
  • After a few months, he worked part-time as an LSAT tutor and also as a freelance writer. He once stated that he started to blog due to boredom.
  • In 2008, Mystal entered a contest at Above the Law (ATL), a news website dedicated to discussing and covering the law, law schools, and the legal profession.
  • The contest was said to be an “American Idol” like competition. The former lawyer essentially won the contest and was later hired by the news agency.
  • He now is the Managing Editor at ATL.
  • Along with his job at ATL, he is the Editor at Large at Breaking Media and is a guest and contributor to major news organizations such as the New York Times and MSNBC.
  • Mystal is now considered one of the most powerful voices in the world of political blogging.
