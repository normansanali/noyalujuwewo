All Dead By Daylight 2023 Anniversary Items And Offerings

August 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Dead by Daylight's 2023 Twisted Masquerade event introduces new items and offerings. Here's all of them.

First Published June 21, 2023, 22:54

Dead by Daylight's Twisted Masquerade 2023 event starts on 22nd June, and with it come new offerings and the return of some fan-favorite anniversary items that survivors can bring with them into trials. Of course, the anniversary marks the return of the annual anniversary offering, which grants over 100% Bloodpoints to all players on both sides and can allow players to earn Bloodpoints quickly.

Here are all of the special event offerings and items that will be available in the Bloodweb during the 2023 Twisted Masquerade event in Dead by Daylight.

Table of Contents

Dead By Daylight Twisted Masquerade 2023 Offering

The event offering for Dead by Daylight's 2023 Twisted Masquerade is the Terrormisu. It will grant 107% bonus Bloodpoints for all players, including both killers and survivors. These offerings stack, so if all survivors and the killer play one, players can earn up to 535% (5.35x) Bloodpoints per trial! This makes playing during the anniversary - and stacking up those Terrormisus - one of the fastest ways to earn Bloodpoints in Dead by Daylight.

Dead By Daylight Twisted Masquerade 2023 Items

Along with the event offering - the Terrormisu - which is available to both killers and survivors, survivors also have some limited-edition event items they can purchase and bring along. Here's a list of the event items available in the Bloodweb during the 2023 Twisted Masquerade:

  • Masquerade Flashlight
  • Masquerade Med-Kit
  • Masquerade Toolbox

These items are returning from last year's Twisted Masquerade 2022 event, which celebrated the game's 6th anniversary and also allowed players to collect Masquerade Masks for killers and survivors.
