Colt Knost was born on 26th June 1985 in Garrettsville, Ohio, United States. With this, he is currently 36 years old and he is American by nationality. Likewise, he is a Christian from a religious point of view and his birth sign is Cancer.

Moving on to his family members, he was born to his father Don Paul, and his mother Luann Knost. His father died in a car accident when he was only 19 months old. Therefore, he never got the chance to recognize him.

However, he later transformed his name into Colt Christopher Paul Knost in his dad’s honor. Similarly, he moved to Texas with his family at the age of 4. His mother Luann was also a golfer but not a professional. This is how he got an interest in golf. He started playing golf at the age of 12.
