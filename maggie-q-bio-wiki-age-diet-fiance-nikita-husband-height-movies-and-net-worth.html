Maggie Q Biography

Maggie Q born Margaret Denise Quigley, is an American actress, animal rights activist as well as a model well-known for starring in the action films Mission: Impossible III and Live Free or Die Hard. She also played the title role of The CW’s action-thriller series Nikita.

Maggie Q Education

Maggie attended Mililani Waena Elementary School and Wheeler Intermediate School where she graduated and joined Mililani High School. At Mililani, she was on the cross country, track and field, and swim teams. She won the title of “Best Body” senior year. Maggie graduated from Milalani in the year 1997.

She won an athletics scholarship to a private university and intended to study veterinary science. Her family was unable to assist her financially. She left Hawaii at the end of one school year in hopes of earning enough money so that she could start by the beginning of the following one.

Maggie Q Model

Maggie began modeling in Tokyo at the age of 17 at the suggestion of a friend. This is before making an unsuccessful move to Taipei, and finally trying in Hong Kong. This is where she started going by the name Maggie Q because the Chinese couldn’t pronounce Quigley properly.

Maggie Q Actress

Maggie started her acting career in the TV drama House of the Dragon, which was a huge hit in Asia in 1998. Maggie made her film debut as Anna in the horror film Model from Hell in 2000, and then went on to star as an FBI agent Jane Quigley in the action thriller Gen-Y Cops the same year.

Maggie’s appearance in Gen-Y Cops impressed Jackie Chan so much that she was cast in Manhattan Midnight and Rush Hour 2.

Maggie Q Mission Impossible

Maggie made her Hollywood breakthrough as a co-star in Mission: Impossible III alongside Tom Cruise in 2006. She also played Zhen, the only female member of the IMF team.

Maggie Q Die Hard

Maggie appeared as Mai Linh in the Bruce Willis movie Live Free or Die Hard in 2007, the fourth film in the Die Hard series, and also as Maggie in Balls of Fury. She played fictional Cao Ying, a granddaughter of the warlord Cao Cao in Three Kingdoms: Resurrection of the Dragon in 2008.

It was her first ancient Chinese costume performance. Q also appeared in the drama/thriller movie Deception that same year starring Ewan McGregor and Hugh Jackman, as Tina. She also stars in the video game Need for Speed: Undercover as the seductive lead character, federal agent Chase Linh, the player’s only contact to the Tri-City Police.

Maggie Q Nikita

Maggie became the lead character, an assassin gone rogue, on the CW series Nikita in 2010. The series is based on the 1990 French film of the same name. Since the premiere of the film, Maggie has been incorrectly described as the first Asian American series lead in an American television drama.
