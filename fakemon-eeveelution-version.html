Champeon

Champeon (JP: パンチア Punchia) is a Fighting-type Eeveelution. Its ability is Sheer Force, and its hidden ability is Guts.

It is classified as the Rogue Pokémon.

It evolves from Eevee when levelled up while the player has a Fighting-type Pokémon in the party. Eevee cannot evolve into Champeon if there is a Steel-type or a dual-type Fighting/Steel Pokémon in the party.
