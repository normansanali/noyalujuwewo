Ben Affleck Shows Off Huge Biceps in Tight T-Shirt, Looks Ripped: Pics

July 2024 · 1 minute read

Holy biceps, Batman! Ben Affleck might need to go up a shirt size now that he’s bulking up to play the Caped Crusader. The Gone Girl hunk, 42, was spotted looking more ripped than ever in a tight gray T-shirt on Tuesday, Jan. 6, while having lunch in L.A.’s Brentwood neighborhood. 

Affleck, who is currently filming Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (opposite fellow hottie Henry Cavill as the Man of Steel), gave onlookers a peek at his chiseled abs and bulging biceps in the snug top. He wore his salt and pepper hair short and had plenty of facial scruff. The Oscar winner paired his eye-catching top half with fitted dark wash jeans and navy sneakers. 

“He has put on 30 pounds of muscle and he’s 8 percent body fat,” Affleck’s wife Jennifer Garner said on Live! With Kelly & Michael in October. “He looks pretty good! It’s a little bit like ‘Whoa, who are you?’ It feels like I’m having an affair. But I don’t mind, I’m not complaining.”
